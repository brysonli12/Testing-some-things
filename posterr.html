FAQ: Do I need any computer programming
experience? Nope! At ACM, we intend to
have members without any programming experience
so that we can teach you
about the wonderful world of computer science
How is this different than the Developer's
Guild? The Developer's Guild
prioritizes writing applications and
workshops--purely educational. At
ACM, we are more relaxed and promote
socializing as well as learning together.
What does ACM do at meetings?
At our club, we have speakers
come in to enlighten us with information
not presented in the class. This can
range from iOS development to
game theory to data structure
supplements. If you have a specific
topic you want to learn about,
we will try to find resources and
speakers to teach us! In addition
to this, we will often times have
challenge problems such as programming
interview questions to prepare
ourselves for the future.
